# Aura Monster Lore Explanation
Just who is this aura monster?
The aura monster, aka Subaru Natsuki, is a man with the ability to "return by death". Yes, I mean exactly what I said, he can return to a point before he died if he dies.
Now, what does this have to do with the aura monster? Well, he walks up this very long staircase with some people, but someone mysteriously pushes him off, and because its dark, he cant tell who.
This happens over and over as he tries different things. Eventually, he starts to go crazy and instead resolves to get rid of his friends until he finds the one who pushed him off.
A specific drawing from the author of the manga was taken by a fan, animated, colored in, and had music put over it, turning it into the "aura monster" clip.

the beginning of this part of the story began in re:zero season 4 episode 8.
